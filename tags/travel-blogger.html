Singapore is a comfortable city for life and visiting.

Singapore is a comfortable city for life and visiting. 💬Why? I will give my reasons:
1. "Good climate." Heat is not, humidity in moderation. If it rains, it quickly ends. The sun does not tire. 🌤
2. There are not many cars on the roads🚘. This allows excursion buses to move freely around the city🚌. For example, in Moscow or London at certain times it is better to think whether to go by car or go faster on foot.
3. Taxis are many🚕. They are relatively inexpensive (from the hotel Marina Bay Sands to Clarke Street costs $ 10) and comfortable. ...
