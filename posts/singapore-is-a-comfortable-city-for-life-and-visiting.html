Singapore is a comfortable city for life and visiting. 💬Why? I will give my reasons:
1. "Good climate." Heat is not, humidity in moderation. If it rains, it quickly ends. The sun does not tire. 🌤
2. There are not many cars on the roads🚘. This allows excursion buses to move freely around the city🚌. For example, in Moscow or London at certain times it is better to think whether to go by car or go faster on foot.
3. Taxis are many🚕. They are relatively inexpensive (from the hotel Marina Bay Sands to Clarke Street costs $ 10) and comfortable. Interestingly, only indigenous Singaporeans can work as taxi drivers🤷
4. Very close to the airport 🛩. The trip takes a maximum of 20-30 minutes.
5. The city made a very cozy impression⛲️🌳🌴. All the time, there was a feeling of security ✅
6. Clean, well-groomed streets with almost the newest buildings🆒🆕.
7. Many opportunities for outdoor activities such as running🏃‍♀️🏃‍♂️, cycling🚴‍♂️🚴‍♀️, rollerblading, etc. along the waterfront. (Do not forget only the inventory👈).
8. Many luxury tropical parks 🌴".
9. Well, a very large number of shopping centers 🏢 (by the way, VAT is returned at the airport👈).
