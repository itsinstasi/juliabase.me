Friends 👥👋🏻
asked me: “Hi, I’m following your hashtag. I’ll be in Singapore. Can you suggest us something to do? Do we have to book to go to the infinity pool view?”
✅✅✅✅✅
My recommendations are:
1. be sure to visit infinity pool by Marina Bay Sands. There are always a lot of people hier😏, that’s why try to come early morning or late evening ( night view 👍🏼).
2. look at the necessarily light and laser show in the Marina Bay at 9:00p.m, 10:00 p.m and connect it with a walk (if you like to walk a lot) to Clarke Street along the Singapore River. Of cause you can take River Cruise 🚢 Boot.
On the way you will see Singapore Lion (Symbol of Singapore).
3. Night life (Party time in the Clarke Street )✌🏼💥on Saturdays is better.
4. To have a dinner at Jumbo Seafood (@vit2k thank you😃)
at Riverside Poin (Singapore Chille Crab🦀 👍🏼👍🏼). Not very expensive. Dinner for two 190$ Sing. Chipper like in another restaurant
5. Marina Bay Garden with Light show
6. Sightseeingtour with the bus (red+yellow line).
7. Fountain of Wealth! Very important))!!At excess time from 2:00-4.00 p.m and 6:00-8:00 p.m
Have a great time.
We love 💕 Singapore
